About me

I've played games for as long as I can remember and started making my own games when I was 13 years old. The first programming I did was gameplay and it's what I like the most, but I also love making systems.
RPGs are by far my favourite genre to play, and they happen to have a lot of gameplay systems in them, so I always have a blast making them. Also, even though RPGs are my favourite genre, I still like playing almost every type of game.
Outside of video games, I don't have many interests and the ones I have often are connected to video games in some way. Those interests include building computers, worldbuilding, MTG, D&D, watching movies/youtube and reading books.
